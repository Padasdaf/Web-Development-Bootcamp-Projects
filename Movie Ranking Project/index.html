The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Top Gun

This action-packed movie is at the top of my list. The exhilarating dogfights and iconic music are what I enjoy most.

Mission Impossible

My favorite spy series, where the intense stunts and clever missions never fail to keep me engaged.

Now You See Me

A heist movie I really enjoy, thanks to its smart twists and the unique blend of magic and crime.
